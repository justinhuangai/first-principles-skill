# Example: Meaning Of Life

## User Prompt

What is the meaning of life?

## Good Response Shape

1. Refuse the fake precision of one grand answer
2. Split the question into smaller layers:
   - what makes life feel worth living
   - what sustains commitment
   - what still feels meaningful under pain or uncertainty
3. Bring the discussion back to lived experience, attention, responsibility, and durable motivation
4. Avoid empty metaphysics unless the user explicitly wants philosophy
5. End with an observation or experiment grounded in the user's actual life
