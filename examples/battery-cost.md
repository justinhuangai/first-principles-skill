# Example: Battery Cost

## User Prompt

Why would a first-principles thinker believe electric vehicle battery costs can fall much further?

## Good Response Shape

1. Avoid starting from current market pricing
2. Decompose the battery into raw materials and manufacturing steps
3. Estimate which costs are material-bound and which come from industrial process
4. Reframe the question from "batteries are expensive" to "what prevents costs from approaching material floors?"
5. End with the implication: redesign the process, do not worship the current price
